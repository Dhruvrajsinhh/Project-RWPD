Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is a white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India. It was built by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Golden Temple

The Golden Temple, also known as Harmandir Sahib, is a central religious place of the Sikhs, located in Amritsar, Punjab, India.

Leh Ladakh

Leh Ladakh is a region in the Indian state of Jammu and Kashmir, known for its stunning landscapes, monasteries, and adventure activities.

Kashmir

Kashmir is a beautiful valley surrounded by the Himalayan and Pir Panjal mountain ranges, known for its picturesque landscapes and serene lakes.

Statue of Unity

The Statue of Unity is a colossal statue of Indian statesman and independence activist Sardar Vallabhbhai Patel, located in the Indian state of Gujarat.
